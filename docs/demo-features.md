### How to Bid

**Placing Your First Bid:**
1. Find an auction you're interested in
2. Click "Bid Now" on the auction page
3. Enter your bid amount (must meet minimum requirements)
4. Click "Place Bid" to confirm
5. You'll receive a confirmation email

**Bid Requirements:**
- Your bid must meet or exceed the starting price
- If updating a previous bid, add at least 100 PLN
- All bids are final and legally binding

**Bid Increments:**
| Price Range | Minimum Increment |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Up to 10,000 PLN | 100 PLN |
| 10,001 - 50,000 PLN | 250 PLN |
| 50,001 - 100,000 PLN | 500 PLN |
| Above 100,000 PLN | 1,000 PLN |

**Important Notes:**
- This is a sealed-bid auction: you cannot see other bidders' amounts
- You can update your bid at any time before the auction ends
- Bids placed in the last 5 minutes may extend the auction by 5 minutes

## Understanding Auctions

### How Our Auctions Work

NovaDrive Motors uses a **sealed-bid auction** format:

1. **Browse**: Find vehicles that interest you
2. **Research**: Review all photos, specifications, and condition reports
3. **Bid**: Place your maximum bid privately
4. **Wait**: Monitor the countdown timer
5. **Win**: Highest bid when time expires wins

**What makes our auctions special:**
- **Privacy**: Your bid amount is not visible to other bidders
- **Fair Competition**: No shill bidding or manipulation
- **Transparency**: Full vehicle history and condition disclosed

### Auction Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Active** | Auction is live, you can place bids |
| **Ended** | Auction time has expired |
| **Sold** | Vehicle has been sold and payment completed |
| **Cancelled** | Auction was cancelled by admin |

### Vehicle Sources

We source vehicles from trusted partners:

- **Insurance Companies**: Vehicles from insurance claims
- **Dealers**: Trade-ins and dealer stock
- **Fleet Companies**: Corporate vehicles being retired
- **Leasing Companies**: End-of-lease returns
- **Private Sellers**: Individual vehicle owners
- **Government**: Former government vehicles

### Vehicle Conditions

Each vehicle is rated for condition:

| Rating | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Excellent** | Like new, minimal wear |
| **Good** | Normal wear for age/mileage |
| **Fair** | Some wear or minor issues |
| **Poor** | Significant issues present |
| **Damaged** | Visible accident damage |
| **Salvage** | Severe damage, may be for parts |

### Payment Process

**After Winning an Auction:**

1. **Deposit (24 hours)**
   - Pay 10% of the winning bid
   - Secures your purchase

2. **Full Payment (5 business days)**
   - Pay the remaining balance
   - Bank transfer or approved financing

3. **Vehicle Pickup (10 business days)**
   - Collect from the specified location
   - Or arrange delivery (starting from 500 PLN)

**Late Fees:**
- Storage charges of 50 PLN per day after 10 days
- Failure to pay may result in deposit forfeiture

### Accepted Payment Methods

- Bank transfer (IBAN provided after winning)
- Escrow service for added security
- Approved financing options
